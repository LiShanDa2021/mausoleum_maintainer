"The Spirit Halloween of Comedy!"

The Mausoleum Maintainer is Madison's Only Halloween-Themed Comedian! (As far as he knows.) He'll make your October events at your venue extra spooky with his Crypt-Keeper inspired persona and his macabre 15-minute set -- perfect to get your crowd in a Halloween mood before your concert or comedy show.

He's not particularly raunchy, but he's definitely not for kids. His comedic influences include Neil Hamburger, Neil Hamburger, Stewart Lee, Krusty the Clown and Dril. The Mausoleum Maintainer's material is LGBTQ+ affirming and he does not punch down.
